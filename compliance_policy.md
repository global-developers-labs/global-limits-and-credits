# Global Limits and Credits Policy

## AI Economic Research Access

This document outlines the usage limits and credit system for accessing AI-powered economic research tools.

### 1. Credit System
*   **Monthly Allowance:** Users are granted **110 credits** per month for free.
*   **Top-up Options:** If you exhaust your monthly credits, you can purchase additional credits for approximately **$1 USD**.
*   **Renewal:** The free 110 credits are automatically renewed every month.

### 2. Usage Limits (Global Limits)
To maintain fair access to economic data, the following activity requirements apply:
*   **Activity Requirement:** Users must maintain at least **90 minutes** of active device time on the platform every two days.
*   **Quota Allocation:** If your device remains active for more than 90 minutes, you will be eligible for a data quota approximately every **4 days**.

### 3. Compliance and Agreement
By using the platform, you agree to adhere to these global limits and credit policies.
